About Our Camps

Educamps offers abudant activites for your children including robotics, sports, and creative art projects. From computer programming to teamwork exercises, EduCamps imparts valueable beginning skills for young people interested in engineering and science, as well as children just being introduced to technology.

Our camps are located at some of the best university campuses in the bay area such as Santa Clara Univeristy, San Jose State University, and University of California Berkeley. EduCamps offers both one and two week camp package options at these sites.
